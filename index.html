GPA Calculator

This simple tool lets UW students calculate the GPA as the different Med Schools in Ontario would see it.

To use it, please upload your marks as an excel sheet below.

Note: The columns must have the following names:

  • course: code of the course
  • grade: out of 100
  • weight: between 0.13 and 1 (maybe more)
  • year: 1, 2, 3, or 4
  • term: fall, winter, or spring (all lower case please)
  • yyyy: the year you took this course (ex: 2019)
